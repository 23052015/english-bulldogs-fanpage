Why do we love English Bulldogs?

  • It is the perfect family dog

    Bulldogs love their owners and specially children. They are loyal companions in every situation. Although sometimes a little bit rough and constantly stubborn, they rarely pose a danger to anyone.

  • Urban dog

    An English Bulldog is not a very athlete type of dog. They spent most of their time lying and sleeping.In addition to that, if it is too hot or too cold and wet outside, you will have a hard time in order to get them out of the apartement.

  • They are loyal and have a big heart

    This type of dog is very attached to their owner. Although they can't swiimm, they would jump into the water if their owner does that.
